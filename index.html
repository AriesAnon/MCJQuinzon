My Projects

i-SENSE

i-SENSE is a meat quality detecting device. It uses multiple sensors - temperature, pH, ammonia, and color sensor, to gather information and identify if the meat is fresh or not. It also utilizes a convolutional neural network to identify the type of meat presented. All of the data is then saved to the device’s local database.

CrimeLab

CrimeLab is a website that allows police officers to keep track of their cases and view 3D recreations of crime scenes and autopsies.

Wolfram Art Gallery

Inspired by art galleries around the world, Wolfram is an application that enables art curators to store artworks and keep track of their employee’s information.

Secure, Contain and Protect

The world is in shambles, and this is how you died. Secure, Contain and Protect is a 2D shooter game that allows you to control an agent and fight against zombies.

Power Monitoring System

Want to know which appliances in your house consume the most power? This application shows just that! Manage rooms, list appliances, and see the top 5 appliances that consumes the most power.
